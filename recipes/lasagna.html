Lasagna

How to make restaurant quality lasagna at home.

This classic italian dish should be included in everyone's cooking repertoire. Delicious, and easy to make in large quantities, lasagna can be an excellent dish when you need to feed several people.

In this article I will first explain all the ingredients and tools necessary to cook the dish, and then give a detailed step-by-step process to creating your first lasagna masterpiece.

Ingredients and Cookware

When cooking it is important to have mise en place (all things in their place). Unless otherwise noted you should have all of your ingredients and cookware readily available and measured to the recipes specifications before you begin the cooking process.

Ingredients

  • Sweet italian sausage (1lb)
  • Lean ground beef (3/4 lb)
  • Minced onion (1/2 cup)
  • Garlic cloves (2, crushed)
  • Crushed tomatoes (28oz)
  • Tomato paste (6oz)
  • Tomato sauce (6.5oz)
  • Water (1/2 cup)
  • White sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • Dried basil leaves (1 1/2 teaspoons)
  • Fennel seeds (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Italian seasoning (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt (1 1/2 teaspoons, or to taste)
  • Ground black pepper (1/4 teaspoon)
  • Fresh parsley (4 tablespoons, chopped)
  • lasagna noodles (12)
  • Ricotta cheese (16oz)
  • Egg (1)
  • Mozzarella cheese (3/4Lb, sliced)
  • Parmesan cheese (3/4 cup, grated)

Cookware

  • Large pot for boiling lasagna
  • Dutch oven
  • Mixing bowl
  • Oven
  • Baking dish (9x13)
  • Foil

Directions

Learning to cook can seem daunting at first, but if you've laid out your ingrediants and cookware properly you'll get this without issue.

  1. Step 1

    2. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

  2. Step 2

    3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

  3. Step 3

  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  5. Step 4

    6. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

  6. Step 5

    7. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
